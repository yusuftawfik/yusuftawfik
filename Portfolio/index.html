About Me

As a youth hailing from a middle-class background,

applications, and games. Commencing this odyssey of discovery at the tender age of ten,
I delved into books, utilized learning applications, watched instructional videos, and pursued courses,
all in pursuit of knowledge. Now, at the age of sixteen, I stand enriched,
having accumulated a wealth of expertise in the domains of website creation, deployment, and fortification.

some of which I proudly exhibit below in the projects section.
